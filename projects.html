Functionality of Waterwells Predictor With Decision Trees

Flatiron Data-Science Program | Phase 3 Final Project (May 2023 - June 2023)

Utilizing the Decision Trees machine learning algorithim, I created a model to predict the working condition of water wells in Tanzania, based on their current functionality, to determine well water quality. To make this possible multiple test models were trained and custom encoding and scaling was necessary.

This data-set contains information from Taarifa and the Tanzanian Ministry of Water, and was apart of a Kaggle Competition.

There was a lot of cleaning and EDA to prep for this model, including tokenizing, punctuation removal, removing capital letters, lemmatizing, removing stop words, vectorizing, etc, in order to ensure the model can work with our data.
